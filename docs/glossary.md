# Glossary

*Draft. Source of truth for definitions used in book, spec, and code.*

---

- **Canonical cell** — Minimal form with nine fields: who, did, this, when, confirmed by, if ok, if doubt, if not, status. Carries action, causality, evidence, branching, and state.
- **Ghost Record** — Placeholder with identity for what cannot yet be affirmed but must be preserved. Not null; structured presence of absence.
- **Proof-Carrying Inference** — Each inference step must carry minimal justification for the right to continue.
- **Ghost Load** — Natural unresolved complexity (cost of investigation).
- **Ghost Debt** — Accumulated epistemic liability from unresolved critical absence.
- **Closure** — The right to conclude; earned when critical ghosts are resolved or explicitly accepted.
- **Closure Fraud** — Concluding despite critical unresolved absence.
- **Continuous Verifier** — Component that governs admissibility of each transition, not only final output.
- **Epistemic state** — Formal state comprising claims, evidences, obligations, ghosts, provenance, uncertainty, branches.
- **LogLine** — Protocol instantiation of the canonical cell; grammar of the verifiable act.
